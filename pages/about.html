    About Us

    What we are about

    Welcome to Basotho Agency, your trusted partner in housekeeping and domestic services. Whether you're looking for a full-time maid, a part-time helper, or someone to assist with specific household tasks, we are here to make your life easier, cleaner, and more organized.

    Our Story

    Founded in 2018, Basotho Agency was born out of a desire to provide reliable, professional, and compassionate domestic help to households in Johannesburg and beyond. With a focus on quality service and client satisfaction. Our name, Basotho, is inspired by the proud heritage and hardworking spirit of the Basotho people, who value integrity, respect, and a strong sense of community. This ethos shapes everything we do at Basotho Agency, as we aim to provide trustworthy and skilled domestic workers who treat your home with the utmost care and attention.

    What we do

    At Basotho Agency, we specialize in providing experienced and reliable domestic workers for all your housekeeping needs. Whether you require a maid for daily cleaning, cooking, or childcare, we offer a range of services designed to fit your specific requirements. Each of our domestic workers undergoes a thorough background check, training, and a careful matching process to ensure they meet our high standards of professionalism, reliability, and trustworthiness. We want you to feel confident and secure in the services we provide.

    Why Choose Us

    Trusted Professionals: Our workers are vetted and trained to offer the highest standard of care and service. You can trust that your home is in good hands. Tailored Solutions: Every household is different, and so are your needs. We offer customized housekeeping services to ensure your home runs smoothly. Reliability and Consistency: We understand the importance of dependability, which is why we prioritize consistent and reliable service. You can count on us to be there when you need us. Affordable and Transparent Pricing: We offer competitive rates with no hidden fees. Our pricing is clear and upfront, so you always know what to expect.
