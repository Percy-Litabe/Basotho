    Frequently Asked Questions

    • What types of services do you offer?

      We provide a range of domestic services including house cleaning, cooking, childcare, laundry, ironing, and general housekeeping. We can also offer specialized services like deep cleaning or post-renovation cleaning, depending on your needs

    • Do our maids have work permits?

      All our maids have valid work permits and passports.

    • Are your workers trained and experienced?

      Yes, all of our domestic workers are carefully screened and trained in the relevant areas, including cleaning and childcare. We ensure that each worker has the necessary experience to meet your expectations.

    • What is the cost of hiring a domestic worker?

      The cost varies depending on the type of service, the duration of the work, and the specific requirements. We offer flexible pricing based on hourly rates or fixed packages. Contact us for a personalized quote based on your needs.

    • Are your workers available for full-time, part-time, or temporary assignments?

      Yes! We offer full-time, part-time, and temporary domestic workers based on your needs. Whether you require someone for a few hours a week or full-time assistance, we can provide a tailored solution.

    • Can I request a specific domestic worker?

      Yes, if you’ve worked with a worker before and would like to request them again, we will do our best to accommodate your request. However, availability can vary, so it’s always best to book in advance.
